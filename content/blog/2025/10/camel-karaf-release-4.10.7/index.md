We are pleased to announce the 4.10.7 release of Camel Karaf.

It includes:
* Upgrade to Camel 4.10.7
* fix on the camel-kafka component
* fix on the camel-jms feature
* fix on the camel-cxf bundle to deal with SOAP exchange

For more information, please check the [Release notes](/releases/karaf-4.10.7/).
