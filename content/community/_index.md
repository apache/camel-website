## Mailing List

One way to get help is to use the Camel mailing lists. This allows the entire community to help with your problem.

When posting to the mailing lists, please use plain text mails.
Do not use HTML mails.

## Who Uses Apache Camel

Over 100 companies use Apache Camel in production — from UPS and CERN to SAP and Korean Air.
Commercial products from Red Hat, SAP, Huawei, and Talend are built on Apache Camel.
